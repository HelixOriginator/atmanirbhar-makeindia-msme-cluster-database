# Atmanirbhar-MakeInIndia MSME Cluster Database

MSME cluster performance and supply chain provenance archive supporting Atmanirbhar Bharat and Make in India. Provides verified industrial heritage data to reduce investment risk. Aligned with DPIIT, Ministry of MSME, and Invest India manufacturing capability initiatives.

## Overview

This repository documents India's MSME cluster performance histories and supply chain provenance data to support investment decisions, policy planning, and industrial development. The database serves as an Industrial Heritage and Capability Atlas for India's manufacturing sector.

## Purpose

- **Supply Chain Provenance**: Track manufacturing origins, quality certifications, and production histories
- **MSME Cluster Performance**: Historical data on cluster productivity, innovation, and economic impact
- **Investment Risk Reduction**: Verified capability records for informed decision-making
- **Industrial Heritage Mapping**: Preserve knowledge and guide future industrial policy

## Data Categories

- MSME cluster profiles and performance metrics
- Supply chain provenance and traceability records
- Manufacturing capability assessments
- Historical industrial development timelines
- Quality certifications and compliance records
- Technology adoption and innovation tracking
- Export performance and international partnerships
